## Identity
- type: web-application
- deployment: docker

## Purpose
A local-first CRM and appointment management system for small-to-medium businesses that unifies customer records, one-to-one WhatsApp conversation history, and scheduling workflows in a single interface. It helps operational teams quickly find customer context, manage appointments, and maintain clear communication and audit visibility without complex enterprise tooling.
